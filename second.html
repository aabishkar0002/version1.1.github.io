RIP TO LEGENDARY CREATOR - AKIRA TORIYAMA



Famous Shonen Manga artist Akira Toriyama passed away at the age of 68 years. He breathed his last on March 1, and the cause of his death is reported to be an acute subdural hematoma. The Japanese artist who was the creator of the famous series Dragon Ball was one of the most influential Manga artists globally.

The comic series Dragon Ball was a worldwide sensation. It made its debut in 1984 and followed the life of a boy called Son Goku and his adventurous tales of collecting dragon balls that would eventually help him become powerful and fight evils. Over the years, Dragon Ball has been adapted into anime series, live-action movies, and video games.

In an official statement from the Dragon Ball team, Akira Toriyama’s passing away was announced. The heartbreaking note said, “We are deeply saddened to inform you that Manga creator Akira Toriyama passed away on March 1 due to acute subdural hematoma. It’s our deep regret that he still had several works in the middle of creation with great enthusiasm. Also, he would have many more things to achieve.”
